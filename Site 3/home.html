Aptus Capital Advisors is a Registered Investment Advisor providing investment management services.

We implement two specific investment strategies each with risk management as the starting point of our process. Our approach blends behavioral finance with technical analysis in an effort to produce attractive returns. We manage separate accounts (taxable and retirement) for our clients through our custodian Interactive Brokers. This set up provides clients exposure to our strategies with complete transparency and access to funds at anytime.
