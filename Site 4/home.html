Welcome to HMG

HMG provides Crop Marketing and Commodity Risk Management services with a full service commodities brokerage desk specializing in hedging and options trades.

Our relationship-driven HMG Crop Marketing Team has over 35 combined years of commodity risk management, crop marketing, and commodity research consulting experience.

HMG services include crop marketing consulting, commodity risk management, , planting intentions consulting, and input hedging. The agriculture commodity markets can be volatile, distracting, and difficult to understand. Our consulting services allow you to concentrate on running your business, so you can leave the commodity risk management and crop marketing to us. Our relationship-driven approach is a collaborative effort, and no decisions are made without input from the client.

HMG also specializes in managed futures with an emphasis in agricultural options strategies tailored to each futures investor client's risk parameters.
